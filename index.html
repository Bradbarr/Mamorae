How sharing works

Shared when you decide.

Nothing leaves your vault without your intention. Every release is yours to set, pause, or change.

Now

Share immediately

Send memories, photos, or messages to specific people right now. Perfect for things that shouldn't wait — or for going public while you're still here to enjoy the reaction.

Scheduled

On a future date

A letter for a grandchild's 18th birthday. An anniversary message. A voice note for next Christmas. Scheduled releases arrive exactly when you intended.

When the time comes

After trusted confirmation

Appoint trusted contacts — a family member, a nurse, a close friend. They confirm when the time has come, and Mamorae releases what you prepared, gently and with care.

🌍
Public memorials — on your terms

Your vault — or a curated part of it — can be made public as a tribute page. Share it before you pass if you'd like, or release it after. A place for friends and family to remember you, exactly as you want to be remembered.
