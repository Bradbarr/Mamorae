Security & Trust

Mamorae is built for privacy and restraint. We act as custodians — never owners — of what you entrust to us.

Mamorae does not hold assets or execute transactions. For highly sensitive information, use Sealed Capsules: content held in a way Mamorae can’t read — only store and release.

Questions people ask

Mamorae is designed to be calm and clear. Here’s what matters most.

Is Mamorae a will or a funeral service? ⌄

No. Mamorae helps you store memories, messages, and intentions — and release them in time. Legal documents are separate.

What triggers a release “when the time comes”? ⌄

Releases can require confirmation by trusted contacts. Mamorae doesn’t rush moments that need humanity.

Can Mamorae see my sealed information? ⌄

Sealed Capsules are designed so Mamorae can’t read the contents — only hold and release them to the recipients you choose.

Does Mamorae store bitcoin or passwords? ⌄

Mamorae is non-custodial: it does not hold assets or private keys. It can hold sealed instructions and intent, released carefully in time.

Can I change my mind later? ⌄

Yes. The Vault is designed to be edited over time. You can update recipients, timing, and content whenever you like.

Is this for pets too? ⌄

Absolutely. Pets have their own place in Mamorae — memories, care notes, and gentle releases when people are ready.
